## 1. Introduction

Welcome to 4rm0Byte (“we,” “our,” or “Platform”). We are committed to protecting
your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is handled in a secure
and responsible manner.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your
information when you visit or interact with our Platform.

By accessing or using this Platform, you agree to the terms of this Privacy
Policy.

## 2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

### a. Personal Information

We do not actively require users to provide personal information. However, if
voluntarily provided (e.g., via contact forms or email), it may include:

- Name
- Email address
- Any other information you choose to share

### b. Non-Personal Information

We may automatically collect certain non-identifiable information, including:

- IP address
- Browser type and version
- Device type and operating system
- Pages visited and time spent
- Referring URLs

### c. Technical and Usage Data

To improve performance and security, we may collect:

- Log files and server diagnostics
- Performance metrics
- Error reports
- Cache and storage usage (e.g., via browser APIs)

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

- Operate and maintain the Platform
- Improve performance, usability, and security
- Analyze usage patterns and trends
- Detect and prevent malicious or unauthorized activity
- Respond to inquiries or communications

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

## 4. Cookies and Storage Technologies

We may use cookies and similar technologies, including:

- Browser cookies
- Local storage
- Cache storage (via Service Workers)

These are used to:

- Enhance user experience
- Store preferences
- Improve performance (e.g., offline support via PWA features)

You may disable cookies through your browser settings; however, some features of
the Platform may not function properly.

## 5. Service Workers and Caching

This Platform uses Progressive Web App (PWA) technologies, including Service
Workers, which may:

- Cache website assets for offline use
- Store files locally in your browser
- Improve loading performance

These technologies operate entirely within your browser and do not grant us
direct access to your local files or personal data.

## 6. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your
information, including:

- Secure hosting environments
- Access control mechanisms
- Monitoring for unauthorized access

However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is
100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 7. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services for analytics, hosting, or performance
monitoring.

These third parties may collect data in accordance with their own privacy
policies. We do not control or assume responsibility for their practices.

## 8. External Links

Our Platform may contain links to external websites. We are not responsible for:

- The content of external sites
- Their privacy practices
- Their data collection policies

You are encouraged to review their policies before interacting with them.

## 9. Data Retention

We retain collected information only for as long as necessary to:

- Fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy
- Comply with legal obligations
- Resolve disputes and enforce policies

Non-personal data may be retained for analytical and security purposes.

## 10. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to:

- Access your personal data
- Request correction or deletion
- Restrict or object to processing

To exercise these rights, you may contact us through available communication
channels.

## 11. Children’s Privacy

This Platform is not intended for individuals under the age of 13 (or applicable
age in your jurisdiction).

We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If such data is
discovered, it will be removed promptly.

## 12. Legal Compliance

We may disclose information if required to do so by law or in response to valid

- Law enforcement agencies

Changes will be effective immediately upon posting. Continued use of the
